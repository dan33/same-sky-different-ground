The flowering calendar

What's in flower right now

The Sunshine Coast keeps a flowering calendar you can read on the ground — the wallum ablaze in spring, the ironbarks feeding the winter, Christmas bells at midsummer. Here's what's out today, by the kind of country it grows in. Step through the year, or filter to one habitat.

at its peak in flower Plants flower on the weather, not the date, so a wet year or a recent fire can move things by weeks. Take these as a rough guide. Times are drawn from the book and the published record.
