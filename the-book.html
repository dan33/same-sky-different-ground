The book

Same Sky, Different Ground

A whole-picture natural history of the Sunshine Coast — from the reef to the range — written for the curious: the newcomer, the lifelong local, the student, the bushwalker and the land manager.

It follows one idea down a single slope: how rock becomes soil, how soil and water and fire decide what grows, and how, by learning to read a few honest signs, you can look at a stretch of country and know roughly what it is and what it has been through.
